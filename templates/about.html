About

Learn, Play, and Master ASL with Our Interactive Game!

Welcome to Our ASL Learning Game

Using the YOLO V8 object detection model, players can instantly recognize the English alphabet sign language they display through the camera. This not only reinforces their memory of the letters but also enhances their learning interest through engaging game challenges.

The game is designed with various levels suitable for different skill levels, ensuring that users of all English proficiency levels can benefit. Additionally, the system is developed on the Django framework, providing stable backend support and creating a user-friendly frontend interface.

How to Play

    Tutorial Mode

    • On the left side, you can select English letters, which will display the corresponding sign language images and explanations.
    • On the right side is the recognition area, showing the recognized letters and their recognition accuracy.

    Wordle Mode

    • Players use sign language recognition to play a word-guessing game.
    • The left side displays the word to be guessed, and players have five attempts to guess it.
    • Correct letters in the correct position are highlighted in green; correct letters in the wrong position are highlighted in yellow; incorrect letters are highlighted in gray.
    • A small keyboard at the bottom will show already entered letters in gray, and players can also use the computer keyboard to play.
    • If players register and log in, their score will be based on the number of attempts it takes to guess the word, and rankings will be displayed accordingly.
